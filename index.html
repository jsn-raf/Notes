Quick Notes

A lightweight note-taking utility for quick capture. Clean layout designed for speed and clarity. All content is stored locally on your device. Accessible at any time without setup or accounts. Nothing is shared or synced externally.

Notes are intentionally minimal by design. Optimised for fast access with low distraction. Text formatting is kept simple and predictable. Everything works consistently across sessions. Straightforward and unobtrusive.
